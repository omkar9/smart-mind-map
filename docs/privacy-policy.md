# Privacy Policy - Smart Mind Map

Last updated: 2026-02-11

Smart Mind Map is an offline-first productivity app by **vishnuambika**.

If you have questions, contact: **omkar946@gmail.com**

## 1. Overview
Smart Mind Map lets users create mind maps, store notes, record node voice notes, and export maps as images. The app is designed for users aged 13+.

## 2. Data We Handle
We do not require account registration.

Data created inside the app:
- Mind map titles, nodes, and notes
- Node style data (color, emoji/icon)
- Node voice note metadata and local file paths
- Local app settings (for example last active time)

Storage behavior:
- App data is stored locally on your device using SQLite and app storage.
- No cloud sync is provided by this app.

## 3. Ads and Third-Party SDK
This app uses **Google AdMob** for monetization.

AdMob may process:
- Device identifiers used for advertising
- Basic diagnostics and ad performance data

Reference:
- https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

## 4. Permissions
Microphone permission (`RECORD_AUDIO`) is requested only when you choose to record a voice note.

The app does not request microphone permission for normal browsing or editing.

## 5. How Data Is Shared
We do not sell your personal data.

Data may be processed by Google AdMob according to Google's own policies when ads are requested and displayed.

## 6. Data Retention and Deletion
- Data created in the app remains on your device unless you delete it.
- If you uninstall the app, local app data may be removed by your device.
- You can delete maps and voice notes from inside the app.

## 7. Children's Privacy
This app is intended for users aged 13+ and is not marketed as a child-directed app.

This policy may be updated in future releases. The "Last updated" date will change when updates are made.
